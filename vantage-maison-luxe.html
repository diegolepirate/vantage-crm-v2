Five principles

The way we design

01

Light

Every room is first a choreography of sunrise, noon, dusk. We design the light before the lamps.

02

Space

Emptiness is expensive and underrated. We protect volume the way a musician protects silence.

03

Material

Oak that darkens, linen that crumples, travertine that fingerprints. We love surfaces that keep a record.

04

Memory

Your grandmother's mirror, the chair from the old flat. We build rooms that honour what you already love.

05

Silence

The best interior is the one you stop noticing — because it finally lets you notice yourself.

03 · Process

How a Maison Luxe project unfolds

1

Listen

We start with a six-hour conversation. No samples, no measurements — just who you are, what you've loved, what you've outgrown.

2

Visit

We walk the space at the three worst hours of its day. Bad light reveals more than good furniture ever will.

3

Plan

A 3D walkthrough, a material wardrobe, and a single, stubborn design proposal. No options — we make a decision and defend it.

4

Build

Our artisan network — all within two hours of Paris — executes. We visit the site twice a week and hate shortcuts.

5

Live

We hand over the keys, then come back at six months and two years. A home is a conversation we plan to continue.
