Alexia Mercier

Executive Head Sommelier at Vantage, Alexia draws on fifteen years between Burgundy and Santorini to build a list that pairs Greek assyrtiko with French grands crus. Formerly at Le Bristol in Paris, she joined Vantage to bring together the sea and the vine.

Marco Theodorakis

Executive Chef trained at Arpège in Paris and Sushi Saito in Tokyo, Marco embodies the Vantage philosophy — Japanese rigour, Mediterranean generosity. Every morning he hand-picks the catch at Piraeus port before writing the evening menu.

Léo Vassilakis

Head Bartender at Vantage, Leo cut his teeth at The Connaught in London before coming home to Athens. His list weaves Chios mastiha, Corfu kumquat and Japanese yuzu — cocktails that drink like dishes, and the other way around.

Elena Papadakis

Pastry Chef trained at the Ritz Paris and at the Hattori school in Tokyo, Elena reinvents classic Greek desserts — her matcha baklava and yuzu loukoumades are now the sweet signature of the house.

A Greek escape beneath the sun

Vantage is an invitation to slow down the Greek way — long lunches, bare feet on warm stone, the low hum of cicadas at golden hour. Our tables celebrate the savoury, sun-ripened gastronomy of the islands: wood-fired breads, olive oil pressed at dawn, fish pulled from the Aegean that same morning. And behind every plate, the unmistakable warmth of the Greek people — generous hosts who turn every guest into kin.

Aegean | Tranquillity

Island stillness

White-washed terraces, the deep blue of the Aegean, and a silence only broken by the breeze. Greece knows how to do nothing — beautifully — and we bring that same calm to every Vantage address.

Cuisine | Gastronomy

Savoury feasts

Grilled octopus, saganaki bubbling in cast iron, honey-drenched loukoumades, tomatoes that taste of the sun. Our kitchens honour the savoury, soulful gastronomy that has fed generations on the islands.

Sun | Well-being

Under the Greek sun

More than a light — a way of living. The Greek sun softens the shoulders, warms the heart, and gives food and skin alike that unmistakable Mediterranean glow. Vitamin D, vitamin joy.

People | Philoxenia

Warm hospitality

In Greece, a stranger is simply a friend you haven't met yet. The famed philoxenia — love of the guest — inspires every detail at Vantage: open arms, full tables, and the quiet pride of welcoming you home.

Cyclades | Santorini

Eat in Greece

Perched above the caldera of Santorini, tables are set where the horizon meets the sea. Each dish is a soft ode to the islands — grilled fish, fresh tomatoes, olive oil of the sun — shared slowly as the sky turns pink.

Ionian | Paxos

Live in Greece

On the hidden coves of Paxos, life slows to the rhythm of the cicadas. Turquoise water, warm pebbles, a gentle breeze — here, simply being is enough. Greece teaches us, softly, what it means to truly live.

Dodecanese | Symi

Fall in love with Greece

A paradise beach at Symi, golden hour on the water, laughter drifting from a taverna on the shore. Greece is the kind of place that gently steals your heart — and gives you, in return, a memory you'll carry forever.
