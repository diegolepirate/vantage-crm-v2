Preamble

Vantage is Greek cuisine re-imagined through a Japanese lens — seabass sashimi dressed in Cretan olive oil, tempura of wild zucchini flowers, soba noodles tossed in sea-urchin butter. Every dish is served on the waterfront and built from ingredients picked, caught or fished the same morning.

Restaurants

Our addresses offer a refined yet generous cuisine, served in unique settings. Mediterranean recipes, Greek gastronomy reimagined through a Japanese lens — an invitation to a culinary journey without leaving the coast.

Vantage R.

Vantage unveils its new rooftop perched six storeys above the Aegean — panoramic sea views, décor inspired by fishing villages, an open kitchen on the water.

Events

Hosting private events with Vantage means an unforgettable evening in one of our waterfront addresses. Product launches, weddings, supper clubs — the sea sets the scene.

Programme

More than a set of restaurants, our addresses are living rooms where chefs, fishermen and musicians meet. Tasting menus, chef residencies, seasonal suppers — the calendar is always in motion.
