— The agency

A small house, a long memory, a careful gaze.

Maison Aurelia was founded on a simple idea: that a home is not a transaction but a chapter. We work with a narrow list of clients and a narrower list of properties — never more than we can know by heart.

Every estate in our portfolio has been walked, measured, and photographed by us. We know the light in October. We know the neighbour who plays oud on Friday nights. We know which stonemason restored the courtyard in 1987.

This is how trust is built — slowly, in person, on location.

— How we work

Four quiet steps. No pressure.

Every introduction begins with a conversation. The sales paperwork, when it arrives, arrives last.
01

Listen

A private meeting, no property list. We learn your rhythm, your timeline, your quiet criteria.

02

Curate

A shortlist of four to six homes — half on-market, half private. Each one walked in person.

03

Preview

Quiet visits, with time to sit in the kitchen and watch the afternoon light.

04

Acquire

Legal, fiscal, notarial — handled by our Athens partners. You sign once and we stay on.
