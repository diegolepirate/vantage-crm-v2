## Objection Handling

**"It's a template, not unique"**
→ "With your colours, photos and content, no visitor
can tell. The effects are hand-coded — this isn't Wix."

**"My competitors could buy the same"**
→ "We never sell the same template to agencies in the
same city. And we limit total sales to 30 per template."

**"I prefer WordPress"**
→ "3× faster load speed. Zero plugins to maintain.
Zero security patches. Zero hosting costs for features.
And frankly — there's no visual comparison."

**"It's too expensive for a template"**
→ "A custom-built site like this would be €8,000–15,000
and take 3 months. You're getting the same quality in 48h."

**"I'll have my developer build it"**
→ "Show them this QA report and effects list.
Ask for a quote. Then call us back."

## Demo Process

1. Send the link to `index-demo.html` (password protected)
2. Walk them through the 4 visual styles via the switcher
3. Ask: "Which style feels most like your brand?"
4. They click "Request this style →" — pre-fills contact form
5. Close the call

**Key question to ask:** "Which mood would your ideal clients
associate with your agency?" — they'll have an opinion.
