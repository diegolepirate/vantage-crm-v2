Azur Côtier

Perched on the pebbled shoreline of Nice, Azur Côtier celebrates the flavors of the Mediterranean. Chef Luc Mercier built this kitchen around a simple promise: the day's catch, the region's terroir, and the sea as a dining companion. A boat-to-table philosophy for those who believe the best meals are shared with a view of the horizon.

Our Inspiration

The ocean, the terroir, and the family recipes behind Azur Côtier.

Chef Luc Mercier grew up in a small fishing village outside Nice, where Sunday lunches with his grandmother stretched long into the afternoon. The smell of garlic and rosemary, the sound of the waves, the quiet generosity of a table shared — these are the memories that shaped this restaurant. Every dish at Azur Côtier is a conversation between that childhood kitchen and the sea just beyond the window. We cook with the land, the tide, and the hands of the people who still remember how it used to taste.

By the Sea.

Every morning, our kitchen opens with the tide. Chef Luc Mercier meets the fishermen of the Baie des Anges before dawn, choosing only what the sea offers that day. No freezers, no shortcuts — only the honesty of the catch.

Olive oil from the hills of Provence, vegetables from growers in the Var, wines from coastal Ligurian estates. Azur Côtier is a tribute to the Mediterranean craftsmanship that has shaped this coast for generations.
