Chapter VI · Everyday

Not every car
is a trophy.

Some are simply honest — a 964 Carrera kept correct, a W124 300E from original ownership, a Defender that never knew the motorway. We collect those too.

It happens more often than you'd think

Someone falls in love with a car online, flies out for a viewing, and decides to buy.

Only later do they discover a matching-number engine that isn't. Service records with gaps no one mentioned. A chassis repainted over a repair that was never declared. Fixing it — if it can be fixed — costs months and tens of thousands of euros.

It pays to have the right people by your side from day one. We read the paperwork, speak to the architects of the car when possible, and confirm that what is promised is real. When something is off, we say it. Before you write a cheque.
