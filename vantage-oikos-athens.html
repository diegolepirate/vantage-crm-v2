— Philosophy

Not agents.
Curators.

We believe a home is first a memory — of salt on stone, of whitewashed courtyards warming in October sun, of shutters closing against the meltemi. Our role is to guard that memory for those who seek to belong to it.

Oikos Athens represents fewer than forty residences at any time. Each property passes our five-step provenance, architecture, light, landscape and silence review before entering our portfolio.

Quiet luxury is not a style — it is a standard of restraint.
