The house

Forty-six years of quiet altitude.

Orbital Atelier is a private dealership for orbital craft, sub-orbital sport rockets, and documented flying-disc prototypes — the kind of machines that refuse display plaques and insist on a pilot.

Every acquisition passes a twelve-step certification written in-house: chassis scan, propellant lineage, re-entry delta-V, cabin ergonomics. We do not buy what we will not fly. We do not sell what we would not own.

Four vaults across Canaveral, Tanegashima, Kourou and Baikonur. Six mission controllers on call. A client list closed since 2003.
