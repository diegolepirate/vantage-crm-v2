Our Promise

Our earnings are donated to the protection of marine ecosystems and the safeguarding of forest wildlife across Europe.

Every bottle poured is a tiny, tangible contribution. We partner with ocean-clean collectives and European forest reserves to make sure that something wild gets to stay wild.

The Craft

Our teas are
prepared with care
by a samurai master.

Sensei Hayato studied under the last of the Urasenke ceremonial brewers of Kyoto. Every leaf in our tins passes through his hands — weighed, warmed, and steeped according to a recipe that is four hundred years old and impossible to rush.

“A tea that is hurried is a tea that is wasted. We brew the way the forest grows — slowly, and on purpose.”
~Tee Nou
